Fault report


Summary: BatteryV=24.4 while OnStation=True (Battery voltage < 25V), with warnings: W4 Not Charging, W29 EEPROM CRC Incorrect.

Root cause: Charger or charging contacts are not delivering the expected station voltage (27–28V) causing the battery to remain low, and the internal EEPROM checksum is mismatched requiring motor recalibration or PCB replacement.


Recommended actions


*	Follow the W4 procedure: ensure the lift is centered on the charging station, verify the station supplies 27–28V, clean charging contacts, and inspect the red wires on the main switch; check the charge LED for normal indication.
*	If the station voltage is not 27–28V or the charge LED behavior suggests a faulty charger (often blinking), check charger connections and fuses; replace the charger if defective and then verify that charging voltage rises.
*	Address W29 by calibrating all motors and restarting the system; for an existing installation perform a test run and, if data are stable, recalibrate a single motor to force checksum recalculation. If the EEPROM CRC warning remains, replace the main PCB (UP000056) or contact DeVi-Stairlifts support.


Verification


*	Confirm BatteryV measures ~27–28V or shows a clear rise while OnStation=True and the charge LED indicates normal charging.
*	Confirm W4 and W29 warnings are cleared and perform a test run to verify normal operation after calibration or PCB replacement.
